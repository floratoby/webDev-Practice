Description

Pandan Bay Institute (PBI), Inc. is a private school in Pandan, Antique. Founded in 1947, the school offers programs for high school and for college students. Following the Extended Basic Education program, PBI has a Senior High School (SHS) department offering the GAS, HUMSS, STEM, and TVL strands. Its college department, on the other hand, provides a bachelor's degree course in Information Technology and an Associate course in Computer Technology. Pandan Bay Institute and its programs are duly recognized by the Department of Education (DepEd) and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED
